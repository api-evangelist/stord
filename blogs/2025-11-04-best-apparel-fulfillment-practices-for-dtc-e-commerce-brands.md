Why Apparel Fulfillment Is a Critical Growth Lever Apparel is one of the most sought-after products in e-commerce. From niche streetwear drops to subscription -based basics and luxury Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) brands, apparel represents one of the most competitive online retail categories. Yet, while the apparel itself drives instant customer acquisition, its fulfillment determines whether buyers stick around for the long-term.
