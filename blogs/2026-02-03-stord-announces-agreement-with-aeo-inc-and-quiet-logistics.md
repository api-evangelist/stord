Stord expands its global fulfillment network with addition of Dallas facility. ATLANTA, February 3, 2026 — Stord , The Consumer Experience Company powering commerce across seamless pre-purchase, checkout, delivery, and post-purchase for today’s leading brands, announced today that it has reached an agreement with American Eagle Outfitters Inc. (AEO) to assume the Dallas fulfillment center, previously operated by Quiet Logistics (Quiet).
