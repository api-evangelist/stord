At first glance, fulfillment seems simple: get a product from your warehouse to a customer. But the difference between shipping to a consumer (DTC) and shipping to another business (B2B) is like the difference between driving a car and piloting a cargo ship. One is about speed and agility for a single destination; the other is about handling massive volume, complex coordination, and strict protocols.
