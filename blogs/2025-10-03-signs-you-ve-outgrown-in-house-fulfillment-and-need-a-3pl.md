Why Fulfillment Becomes a Growth Bottleneck Every direct-to-consumer (DTC) brand begins with do-it-yourself (DIY) fulfillment. Founders pack boxes in basements, family members run shipping labels, and a single leased warehouse hums with just enough efficiency to keep up with early growth. But as brands scale, whether through viral campaigns, subscription models, or wholesale expansion, the logistics equation changes.
