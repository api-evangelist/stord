Why Subscription-Based Fulfillment Matters Subscription commerce has exploded across categories like beauty, supplements, apparel, and beverages. Whether it’s a monthly curated box or a replenishment program for everyday essentials, this fulfillment model has offered brands predictable revenue streams and a powerful way to build customer loyalty. But these benefits are heavily dependent on flawless fulfillment capabilities.
