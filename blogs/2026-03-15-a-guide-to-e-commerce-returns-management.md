Returns are much more than just a reverse transaction; it’s a critical part of your entire operation that directly affects customer satisfaction, warehouse efficiency, and your bottom line. When a customer decides to return an item, they kick off a chain of events that touches nearly every part of your business. A well-designed returns process creates a smooth, positive experience for the customer, making them more likely to shop with your brand again.
