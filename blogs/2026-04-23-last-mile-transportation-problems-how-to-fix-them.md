Your shipping budget is one of the trickiest lines on your P&L to control. A huge portion of that expense, often more than half of your total shipping costs, 1 comes from one single stage of the journey. The last-mile delivery is the final, complex, and notoriously expensive trip between a local warehouse to your customer's door.
